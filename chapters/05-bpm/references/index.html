    References: Business Process Management¶

    1. Business process management - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of BPM lifecycle, modeling, automation, and continuous improvement. Anchors the chapter's BPM framing.

    2. Business Process Model and Notation - Wikipedia - Detailed reference for BPMN 2.0 syntax including pools, lanes, gateways, and events. Directly supports the BPMN MicroSim used in this chapter.

    3. Robotic process automation - Wikipedia - Coverage of RPA platforms, use cases, and the boundary between RPA and intelligent automation. Foundation for the RPA section.

    4. Fundamentals of Business Process Management (2nd Edition) - Marlon Dumas, Marcello La Rosa, Jan Mendling, and Hajo A. Reijers - Springer - The most thorough academic textbook on BPM; Chapter 3 on BPMN is the standard classroom reference for the modeling content in this textbook.

    5. The Lean Startup - Eric Ries - Crown Business - Establishes the build-measure-learn loop and process-improvement mindset that underpins modern BPM practice.

    6. BPMN 2.0 Specification - Object Management Group - The official BPMN standard published by OMG. Authoritative reference when MicroSim diagrams or exam questions need to be precise about notation.

    7. Camunda BPMN Tutorial - Camunda - Free, well-illustrated tutorial covering BPMN elements with interactive examples. Excellent supplement to the chapter's modeling content.

    8. Lean Six Sigma Overview - American Society for Quality - Authoritative introduction to Lean and Six Sigma methodologies referenced in the continuous improvement section.

    9. Process Mining: A Practical Introduction - Wil van der Aalst (Process Mining home) - The originator's site for process mining, the data-driven analysis technique that increasingly augments traditional BPM.

    10. Workflow Automation Best Practices - UiPath - Vendor primer on RPA with concrete examples of automated business processes. Useful for grounding the RPA discussion in real implementations.
