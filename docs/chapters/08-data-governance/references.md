# References: Data Governance and Quality

1. [Data governance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_governance) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of data governance principles, frameworks, and roles including data stewards and chief data officers. Anchors the chapter's governance framing.

2. [Master data management](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Master_data_management) - Wikipedia - Detailed coverage of MDM architectures, golden-record creation, and identity reconciliation. Foundation for the chapter's MDM section.

3. [Data quality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_quality) - Wikipedia - Discussion of data quality dimensions (accuracy, completeness, timeliness, consistency, validity) used throughout this chapter's frameworks.

4. The DAMA Guide to the Data Management Body of Knowledge (DAMA-DMBOK2) - DAMA International - Technics Publications - The authoritative reference for data governance, stewardship, lineage, and quality; the source most data governance certifications draw from.

5. Data Governance: How to Design, Deploy and Sustain an Effective Data Governance Program (2nd Edition) - John Ladley - Academic Press - Practitioner-focused guide on standing up real governance programs; especially useful for the chapter's policy and operating-model content.

6. [DAMA Wheel and DMBOK Framework](https://www.dama.org/cpages/body-of-knowledge) - DAMA International - Authoritative description of the eleven knowledge areas in the DAMA wheel referenced in this chapter.

7. [Data Lineage Best Practices](https://www.ibm.com/topics/data-lineage) - IBM - Clear vendor-neutral explanation of data lineage, its automation, and its role in trust and compliance. Directly supports the lineage section.

8. [Open Data Quality Framework](https://datagovernance.com/the-data-governance-institute-framework/) - Data Governance Institute - Free reference framework covering the rules, decision rights, and accountabilities that constitute a working governance program.

9. [Six Dimensions of Data Quality](https://www.dataversity.net/the-six-dimensions-of-data-quality/) - DATAVERSITY - Practical breakdown of the six commonly cited data-quality dimensions with concrete examples and measurement techniques.

10. [Privacy by Design Principles](https://iapp.org/resources/article/privacy-by-design-the-7-foundational-principles/) - International Association of Privacy Professionals - Cavoukian's seven foundational principles, central to the privacy-meets-governance discussion later in this chapter.
