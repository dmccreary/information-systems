# References: Business Process Management

1. [Business process management](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Business_process_management) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of BPM lifecycle, modeling, automation, and continuous improvement. Anchors the chapter's BPM framing.

2. [Business Process Model and Notation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Business_Process_Model_and_Notation) - Wikipedia - Detailed reference for BPMN 2.0 syntax including pools, lanes, gateways, and events. Directly supports the BPMN MicroSim used in this chapter.

3. [Robotic process automation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robotic_process_automation) - Wikipedia - Coverage of RPA platforms, use cases, and the boundary between RPA and intelligent automation. Foundation for the RPA section.

4. Fundamentals of Business Process Management (2nd Edition) - Marlon Dumas, Marcello La Rosa, Jan Mendling, and Hajo A. Reijers - Springer - The most thorough academic textbook on BPM; Chapter 3 on BPMN is the standard classroom reference for the modeling content in this textbook.

5. The Lean Startup - Eric Ries - Crown Business - Establishes the build-measure-learn loop and process-improvement mindset that underpins modern BPM practice.

6. [BPMN 2.0 Specification](https://www.omg.org/spec/BPMN/2.0/) - Object Management Group - The official BPMN standard published by OMG. Authoritative reference when MicroSim diagrams or exam questions need to be precise about notation.

7. [Camunda BPMN Tutorial](https://camunda.com/bpmn/) - Camunda - Free, well-illustrated tutorial covering BPMN elements with interactive examples. Excellent supplement to the chapter's modeling content.

8. [Lean Six Sigma Overview](https://asq.org/quality-resources/six-sigma) - American Society for Quality - Authoritative introduction to Lean and Six Sigma methodologies referenced in the continuous improvement section.

9. [Process Mining: A Practical Introduction](https://www.processmining.org/) - Wil van der Aalst (Process Mining home) - The originator's site for process mining, the data-driven analysis technique that increasingly augments traditional BPM.

10. [Workflow Automation Best Practices](https://www.uipath.com/rpa/robotic-process-automation) - UiPath - Vendor primer on RPA with concrete examples of automated business processes. Useful for grounding the RPA discussion in real implementations.
