# References: AI Productivity Impact on IS Operations

1. [GitHub Copilot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GitHub_Copilot) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive coverage of the dominant AI coding assistant including capabilities, controversies, and productivity studies. Foundation for the chapter's AI-in-development content.

2. [AIOps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIOps) - Wikipedia - Detailed treatment of AI for IT operations including anomaly detection, alert correlation, and automated remediation. Anchors the chapter's AIOps section.

3. [Chatbot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chatbot) - Wikipedia - Coverage of conversational AI evolution from rule-based to LLM-powered virtual agents. Supports the chapter's help desk content.

4. The AI-Powered Enterprise - Seth Earley - Lifetree Media - Practitioner reference on AI-driven productivity at the enterprise level, with concrete frameworks for measuring uplift; directly aligned with the chapter's measurement content.

5. Co-Intelligence - Ethan Mollick - Portfolio - Accessible, research-grounded book on working effectively with AI; particularly strong on the change-management dimensions of AI-augmented teams.

6. [GitHub Copilot Productivity Research](https://github.blog/2022-09-07-research-quantifying-github-copilots-impact-on-developer-productivity-and-happiness/) - GitHub - The original research study on Copilot's measured impact on developer productivity. Required reading for the chapter's measurement content.

7. [GitHub Copilot Documentation](https://docs.github.com/en/copilot) - GitHub - Authoritative documentation for the AI coding assistant including governance, content exclusions, and audit log capabilities.

8. [DORA State of DevOps Report](https://dora.dev/research/) - DORA / Google - Annual research on software delivery performance including the impact of AI tools on the four key DORA metrics. Pairs with the chapter's productivity-measurement content.

9. [ServiceNow Now Assist for ITSM](https://www.servicenow.com/products/now-assist.html) - ServiceNow - Vendor reference for AI assistance embedded in the dominant ITSM platform. Concrete example for the chapter's AIOps and help-desk content.

10. [McKinsey State of AI Report](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai) - McKinsey - Annual industry survey on AI adoption, productivity uplift, and ROI patterns. Useful for the chapter's evaluate-level outcomes on productivity claims.
