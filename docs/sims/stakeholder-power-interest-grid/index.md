## About This MicroSim

The classic stakeholder map: **Power** on the y-axis, **Interest** on the x-axis. Twelve realistic IS-project stakeholders are pre-positioned. Drag any dot to reposition them; the side panel updates with the recommended engagement strategy (Manage Closely, Keep Satisfied, Keep Informed, Monitor). Click **What-If: Public Incident** to see how a regulatory incident shifts compliance/auditor interest and erodes sponsor power.

## Lesson Plan

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this activity, students will be able to:

1. Place stakeholders on a power/interest grid
2. Justify the placement
3. Select an appropriate engagement strategy for each quadrant
